## Voice & Tone Guidelines

- **Professional but approachable.** Write like a knowledgeable neighbor who runs a cleaning business.
- **Confident without arrogance.** State what you do well. Back it up with specifics.
- **Direct and clear.** Short sentences. No jargon. No filler.
- **Action-oriented.** Every section nudges toward a phone call or form submission.
- **Locally grounded.** Reference Atlanta, Georgia, Metro Atlanta, and specific cities.
- **No exclamation point overuse.** One per section maximum.
